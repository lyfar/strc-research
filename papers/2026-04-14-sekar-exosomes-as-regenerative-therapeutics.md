## What they found
Non-scarring alopecias, including androgenetic alopecia and alopecia areata, are common hair loss disorders with significant psychosocial impact. Conventional treatments such as minoxidil, finasteride, corticosteroids, and platelet-rich plasma (PRP) often demonstrate variable efficacy, relapse rates, or adverse effects. Emerging interest in regenerative dermatology has positioned exosome-based therapy as a potential next-generation solution.
