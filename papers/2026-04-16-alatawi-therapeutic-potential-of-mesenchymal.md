## What they found
Endocrinologists and nephrologists continually seek new therapeutic approaches for diabetic nephropathy (DN). Lately, mesenchymal stem cell (MSC)-derived exosomes (MSC-EXs) have been applied as natural carriers for targeted drug delivery. Thus, we aim to estimate the therapeutic potentialities of MSCs-EXs loaded with ordinary selenium (Se) or its nanoform (NSe) as a promising cell-free therapy in diabetic nephropathy (DN). Eighty rats were randomly split into 8 groups; control, EXs, EXs&#x2009;+&#x2009;Se, EXs&#x2009;+&#x2009;NSe, diabetic (D), D&#x2009;+&#x2009;EXs, D&#x2009;+&#x2009;EXs&#x2009;+&#x2009;Se, and D&#x2009;+&#x2009;EXs&#x2009;+&#x2009;NSe groups.
