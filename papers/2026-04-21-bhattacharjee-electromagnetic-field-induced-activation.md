BackgroundAmyloid-&#x3b2; accumulation and misfolding are key features of Alzheimer's disease (AD), contributing to synaptic dysfunction, neuroinflammation, and neuronal loss. In AD, these mechanisms become impaired, leading to toxic aggregation.
